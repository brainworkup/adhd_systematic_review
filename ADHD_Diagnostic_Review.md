## Test Description

The MMPI-2 (Minnesota Multiphasic Personality Inventory-2) is used to assess validity through scales such as:
- Infrequency-Psychopathology (Fp)
- Fake Bad Scale (FBS)
- Response Bias Scale (RBS)
- Henry-Heilbronner Index (HHI)

The test helps detect individuals attempting to feign ADHD symptoms, with the Fp scale showing the best balance of sensitivity (59%) and specificity (94%) at a cutoff of â‰¥5.
